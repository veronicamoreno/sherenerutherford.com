Synopsis

Robin Sparrow and his friend, Porcupine Pete, want to visit Kingston City to have an adventure. On the way, they meet a dog who lives in an orphanage.
The weather means that their adventure isn't quite what they planned, but they've made a new friend.
A story of fun and friendship, with charming illustrations, for very young readers.
