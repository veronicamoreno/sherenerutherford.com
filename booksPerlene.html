A Journey for Perlene.

Jumping out of the frying pan and into the fire.

Synopsis

Perlene can remember vaguely that her childhood was happy whilst her mother resided in Jamaica but then she left to join her sick mother in England with the promise to Perlene and her two sisters to be reunited with her in the near future.' The heartwarming story of three barrel children from Jamaica who were reunited with their mother in England. Antoinette, who made a good life for herself. Camille, who found it difficult to free herself of her childhood demons. And Perlene, a true survivor with a fighting instinct which ensured that she never gave up when life got extremely tough. Despite the dark themes of abuse, suicide and divorce, this story is an uplifting one, full of love, determination, faith and forgiveness alongside hopes and dreams for the future.
